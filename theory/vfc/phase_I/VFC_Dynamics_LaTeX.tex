\section*{Vortex Field Compression Dynamics}

Let $\Psi_s$ denote a spin-aligned field carrier and $\phi_h$ the harmonic phase velocity.
The vortex pressure fold resonance is defined by:
\begin{equation}
\Lambda_r = \left( \frac{\phi_h \cdot \omega_c}{R_v} \right) \cdot \cos(\theta_{lock})
\end{equation}

Where:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $\omega_c$ is the angular frequency of compression
  \item $R_v$ is the radial pressure differential of the vortex
  \item $\theta_{lock}$ is the alignment phase angle during compression
\end{itemize}

Equilibrium is achieved when:
\begin{equation}
\frac{d\Lambda_r}{dt} \rightarrow 0
\end{equation}
